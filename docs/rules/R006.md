# R006 — Icing Conditions

## Real-World Failure Mode

Aircraft icing is one of the most dangerous weather-related hazards. Ice accumulation on wings, tail surfaces, and propellers degrades aerodynamic performance, increases stall speed, and adds weight. Pitot tube icing causes airspeed indicator failure. Structural icing can accumulate rapidly in certain conditions (freezing rain, supercooled large droplets) and has caused numerous fatal accidents.

## How the Rule Works

R006 monitors three distinct icing conditions:

1. **Structural ice accumulation**: Directly reads the SimConnect structural ice percentage. Escalates severity based on accumulation level.
2. **Pitot ice accumulation**: Reads pitot ice percentage for airspeed system integrity.
3. **Icing envelope detection**: Checks if OAT is within the icing temperature range (from profile) AND the aircraft is in visible moisture (cloud or precipitation). If the aircraft has pitot heat capability and pitot ice is detected, fires an advisory to remind the pilot.

## Severity Levels

| Severity | Condition |
|----------|-----------|
| CRITICAL | Structural ice >= 25% OR Pitot ice >= 25% |
| WARNING | Structural ice >= 5% |
| ADVISORY | In icing envelope + pitot ice >0.5% (pitot heat reminder) |

## Applicability

- Airborne only (not applicable in Ground or Landing phases)
- All aircraft types

## Key Thresholds

- Structural ice warning: 5%
- Structural ice critical: 25%
- Pitot ice critical: 25%
- OAT icing range: `profile.icing.icing_oat_range_c` (e.g., -20 to +2 C)
- Moisture required: in cloud OR precipitation active
- Pitot ice advisory trigger: >0.5%

## Required SimVars

- `StructuralIcePct`
- `PitotIcePct`
- `AmbientTemperature`
- `AmbientInCloud`
- `AmbientPrecipState`

## Evaluation Interval

10 seconds

## Limitations

- MSFS icing model is simplified compared to real-world conditions
- Structural ice percentage in MSFS does not distinguish between rime, clear, and mixed ice
- Some aircraft add-ons may have custom icing implementations that don't update standard SimVars
- Cannot detect supercooled large droplet (SLD) conditions specifically
