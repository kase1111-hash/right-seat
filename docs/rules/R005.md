# R005 — Fuel Imbalance

## Real-World Failure Mode

Fuel imbalance between left and right tanks creates asymmetric weight distribution, requiring constant trim correction and reducing controllability margins. In severe cases, one tank may run dry while the other still holds fuel, risking engine fuel starvation on the dry side. Fuel imbalance typically develops from stuck fuel selectors, leaking fuel caps, asymmetric fuel return lines, or pilot inattention to fuel management.

## How the Rule Works

R005 compares fuel quantities between tank pairs (left/right) and monitors the trend:

1. **Per-tank minimum check**: Any individual tank below the profile's minimum fuel threshold while other tanks have fuel triggers CRITICAL.
2. **Percentage imbalance**: Computes the difference between left-side and right-side fuel as a percentage of total fuel.
3. **Trend-aware advisory**: At the advisory level, the alert only fires if the imbalance is actively growing (rate-of-change positive over 120 seconds). This prevents alerting on stable, intentional imbalances.

## Severity Levels

| Severity | Condition |
|----------|-----------|
| CRITICAL | Any tank below minimum while others have fuel |
| WARNING | Imbalance >= warning threshold percentage |
| ADVISORY | Imbalance >= advisory threshold AND actively growing |

## Applicability

- Aircraft with 2+ fuel tanks
- All phases
- Requires total fuel >1.0 gallon and tank pair total >2.0 gallons

## Key Thresholds

- Advisory threshold: `profile.fuel.imbalance_advisory_pct`
- Warning threshold: `profile.fuel.imbalance_warning_pct`
- Minimum fuel per tank: `profile.fuel.minimum_fuel_warning_gal`
- Total fuel minimum: >1.0 gallon
- Tank pair minimum: >2.0 gallons
- Trend window: 120 seconds

## Required SimVars

- `FuelSystemTankQuantity` (per tank)

## Evaluation Interval

10 seconds

## Limitations

- SimConnect fuel quantities may not precisely match real fuel gauges in all add-on aircraft
- Cannot detect the cause of imbalance (leak vs. selector issue vs. fuel return asymmetry)
- Trend detection requires 120 seconds of data before advisory can fire
